Advancing Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence

The Berkeley Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI) is a multidisciplinary initiative dedicated to advancing the science, technology, and education of decentralization and AI to empower a responsible digital economy.

Research

Advancing next-generation decentralized technologies and responsible AI, focusing on fundamental challenges in scalability, usability, provable security guarantees, auditable privacy, incentive compatibility, systemic stability, and autonomous intelligence. Our work spans secure decentralized architectures, multi-agent systems, AI safety, and governance frameworks - creating robust foundations for trustworthy AI and decentralized systems.

Advancing Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence

The Berkeley Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI) is a multidisciplinary initiative dedicated to advancing the science, technology, and education of AI and Agentic AI to empower a responsible digital economy.

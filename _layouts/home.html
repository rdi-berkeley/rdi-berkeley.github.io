RDI stands as a hub for global innovation and collaboration. We have launched several pioneering educational initiatives that have garnered worldwide participation. At the forefront is our world-first LLM Agents MOOC, which has drawn tens of thousands of registered learners. Additionally, we have also launched the world's first Zero Knowledge Proofs MOOC and Decentralized Finance MOOC, with each attracting thousands of students globally. These educational initiatives are designed to democratize access to cutting-edge knowledge across diverse domains. In addition, in alignment with our commitment to open-source principles, work funded by RDI is generally released into the public domain through publications and open-source code.

For more details about the RDI Center and to stay updated on our latest developments, please join our mailing list. Students interested in exploring research opportunities and internship programs offered by the RDI Center are encouraged to fill out this form. If you have questions about our academic offerings, please visit RDI Academics for more details.

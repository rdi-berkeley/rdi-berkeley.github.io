ZKP/Web3 Career Fair & Developer Info Site

May 2nd, 2023

Come land an exciting job or internship at a rapidly growing company in Web3 & Blockchain.

We’re bringing together 18 industry-leading companies who are looking to fill over hundreds job and internship positions. You won’t want to miss this opportunity!

Also, come check out the ZKP / Web3 Hackathon, hosted by Berkeley RDI in partnership with ZKP MOOC.

Date and Location

May 2, 2023 5pm-8pm
Wozniak Lounge | Soda Hall, UC Berkeley

Registration

Registration for this event will be free.

Note that limited in-person seating available, and ticket sales for in-person will close once capacity is reached.

This event will also be live-streamed on our RDI YouTube - select virtual attendance for your ticket type to be sent the the livestream link!

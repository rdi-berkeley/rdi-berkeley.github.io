Agentic AI Summit 2025 - A Record-Breaking Gathering at UC Berkeley!


We are thrilled to share the highlights from this year’s Agentic AI Summit at UC Berkeley! The energy and enthusiasm were off the charts, with over 2,000 attendees joining us in person and ~40,000 participants tuning in from around the world via our livestream.

The summit brought together visionary leaders, innovators, builders and entrepreneurs from every corner of the globe—all united by a shared passion for the future of agentic AI. The buzz and engagement throughout the event were a testament to our community’s excitement about the possibilities ahead.


Missed the summit or want to revisit your favorite moments? All sessions are now available to watch!


👉 Catch the recordings here .


A heartfelt thank you to everyone who joined us onsite and online, and to our esteemed speakers, sponsors, and volunteers who made this event possible. Your passion and commitment are what make this community so special. The future of AI is agentic—and we’re just getting started!





If you enjoyed the summit, please help us grow our community by sharing our posts about the event on Twitter/X and LinkedIn .


We’re also excited to announce that the Agentic AI Summit will return next year! Details are coming soon. Thank you for being part of this landmark gathering—we can’t wait to see you again and continue building the future of agentic AI together.

AgentX Competition: Unprecedented Global Participation and Winning Teams Announced

We’re thrilled to share the incredible success of the AgentX Competition . This year saw record-breaking participation, with nearly 1,000 teams competing across both the Entrepreneurship and Research tracks—making AgentX one of the largest AI agent competitions ever held.


The competition united an extraordinary community of builders, developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs, all working to advance the frontiers of LLM agent technology. Teams submitted innovative solutions spanning enterprise software, developer tools, consumer applications and pioneering research in agent architecture, AI safety, reasoning &planning, benchmark &evaluation and multi-agent systems.

🚀 Announcing the Agentic AI MOOC — Fall 2025!


We’re excited to invite you to join the rapidly growing global community of over 25,000 learners in the upcoming Agentic AI MOOC , launching this Fall 2025!


Agentic AI is transforming the future of intelligent automation and personalization. This MOOC will guide you through the foundations and frontiers of Agentic AI, building upon the success of our previous LLM Agents MOOCs. Whether you’re a student, researcher, developer, or simply curious about the next wave of AI, this is your launchpad.


What You’ll Learn

  • Essential Concepts: Foundations of LLMs, reasoning, planning, agentic frameworks, infrastructure, etc.                

  • Hands-On Applications: Explore real-world use cases in code generation, robotics, web automation, scientific discovery, etc.


Course Details

  • Lecture Time: Mondays, 3-5pm PT (starting September 6, 2025)

  • Format: Online , open to a global audience

  • The full syllabus will be announced soon—stay tuned!

Decentralization and AI Workshop

Aug 27, UC Berkeley

The Decentralization and AI workshop aims to gather researchers, students, practitioners, and entrepreneurs in academia and industry interested in the latest advancements, challenges, and applications at the intersection of decentralization and AI. The workshop aims to provide an overview of the state-of-the-art development of this field, discuss current deployment of AI in various blockchain applications and highlight specific challenges and open problems that arise in these settings.

The workshop is hosted by Berkeley RDI, a multi-disciplinary campus-wide center, focusing on advancing the science, technology and education of web3, decentralization and empowering of a responsible digital economy.

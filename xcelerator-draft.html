Why Berkeley Xcelerator

The Berkeley Xcelerator is a joint initiative of Berkeley RDI, Haas School of Business, and SCET designed to help founders develop breakthrough ideas into scalable, venture-backable companies.

Unlike traditional accelerators, the Berkeley Xcelerator is completely non-dilutive and free for selected startups.

Berkeley Xcelerator

The Berkeley Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI) is building the global hub for innovation in AI and Agentic AI. At the heart of this mission is the Berkeley Xcelerator — a joint initiative of Berkeley RDI, the Haas School of Business, and the Sutardja Center for Entrepreneurship & Technology (SCET) — designed to help founders turn breakthrough ideas into scalable, venture‑backable companies that shape the future of intelligent systems.

Why Berkeley Xcelerator

The Berkeley Xcelerator is a joint initiative of Berkeley RDI, Haas School of Business, and SCET designed to help founders develop breakthrough ideas into scalable, venture-backable companies.

Unlike traditional accelerators, the Berkeley Xcelerator is completely non-dilutive and free for selected startups.

What We Offer

Unparalleled Research Access

Plug directly into Berkeley's frontier research at the intersection of Agentic AI Foundation, AI Safety & Security, and related domains, giving companies an early advantage grounded in scientific excellence.

Elite Mentorship & Advisors

Founders receive guidance from renowned faculty, serial entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and top venture investors with experience scaling successful companies.

Global Innovation Community

Startups access Berkeley RDI's network of 32,000+ builders, developers, researchers, and founders worldwide collaborating on responsible AI advancement.

Market Visibility & Validation

Companies showcase their work at summits and demo days to build credibility with investors, customers, and enterprise partners.

Cloud & GPU Resources

Participants receive cloud credits, GPU access, and computational support from major hyperscalers, enabling efficient model development and deployment.

Why Berkeley Xcelerator

The Berkeley Center for Responsible, Decentralized Intelligence (RDI) is building the global hub for innovation in AI and Agentic AI. At the heart of this mission is the Berkeley Xcelerator, designed to help founders turn breakthrough ideas into scalable, venture‑backable companies that shape the future of intelligent systems.

Unlike traditional accelerators, the Berkeley Xcelerator is completely non-dilutive and free for selected startups.

ArmorIQ

ArmorIQ is an AI agent security platform that acts as a control fabric between autonomous agents and enterprise governance domains, intercepting actions in real time and enforcing intent-based policy before any API call, data access, or workflow executes. Unlike traditional IAM or guardrails, ArmorIQ addresses the emerging threat of agents acting outside their delegated scope, blocking rogue behavior at the reasoning-to-execution boundary with full audit trails for SOC2, GDPR, and NIST compliance.

What We Offer

Unparalleled Research Access

Plug directly into Berkeley's frontier research at the intersection of Agentic AI Foundation, AI Safety & Security, and related domains, giving companies an early advantage grounded in scientific excellence.

Elite Mentorship & Advisors

Founders receive guidance from renowned faculty, serial entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and top venture investors with experience scaling successful companies.

Global Innovation Community

Startups access Berkeley RDI's network of 40,000+ builders, developers, researchers, and founders worldwide collaborating on responsible AI advancement.

Market Visibility & Validation

Companies showcase their work at summits and demo days to build credibility with investors, customers, and enterprise partners.

Cloud & GPU Resources

Participants receive cloud credits, GPU access, and computational support from our industry partners to accelerate product development.

  • Up to $10,000 in API credits from OpenAI
  • Up to $10,000 in Google Cloud and Gemini credits from Google Cloud & DeepMind
  • Up to $10,000 in compute/inference credits from Nebius
  • More cloud, GPU, and infrastructure support to be announced

What You'll Achieve

By the end of the Xcelerator cohort, teams are expected to demonstrate meaningful progress across the following areas:
  • Clearly Defined Problem & Target Customer
    A well-articulated problem statement, a clearly identified target customer profile, and a validated use case.
  • Working Product Demo
    A functional product demo (from MVP to early v1) showcasing core capabilities.
  • Early Validation & Traction
    Tangible evidence of validation, such as user engagement, pilot programs, customer feedback or other credible signals of demand.
  • Go-to-Market Strategy
    Clear plan for distribution, scaling, user/customer acquisition, and early revenue.

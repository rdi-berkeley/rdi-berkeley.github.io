Announcement:

Check out our Agentic AI MOOC series with almost 40,000 learners worldwide!

Welcome to the Agentic AI Summit 2026, hosted by the Berkeley Center for Responsible Decentralized Intelligence and the Responsible Decentralized Intelligence Foundation.

Building on the momentum of our popular Agentic AI MOOC series—now a rapidly growing global community of almost 40 thousand learners—we're excited to invite you to join us on August 1–2, 2026 for a two-day summit on the beautiful UC Berkeley campus.

The 2026 summit will continue and expand the vision of 2025: bringing together visionary leaders from academia, pioneering entrepreneurs, experts from leading AI organizations, venture capitalists, and policymakers to explore, discuss, and collaboratively shape the future of AI and Agentic AI. Our goal is not merely to showcase the latest advancements, but to build bridges across communities and catalyze meaningful conversations and partnerships.

Together, we'll navigate the path toward Agentic AI that doesn't just automate tasks, but actively empower individuals, enrich societies, and amplify human potential—helping lay the groundwork for a robust, safe, and transformative AI-powered future.

For 2026, we expect 5,000+ in-person attendees and many thousands of livestream participants joining us for this landmark event.

Dates:   August 1–2, 2026

Venue:   UC Berkeley Campus, Berkeley, CA

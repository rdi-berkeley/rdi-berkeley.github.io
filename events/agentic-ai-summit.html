Welcome to the Agentic AI Summit 2025, hosted by Berkeley Center for Responsible Decentralized Intelligence! On August 2nd, on the beautiful Berkeley campus, we will bring visionary leaders from academia, pioneering entrepreneurs, experts from leading AI organizations, venture capitalists, and policymakers to explore, discuss, and collaboratively shape the exciting future of AI and Agentic AI. This summit aims not merely to showcase the latest advancements, but to build bridges across constituencies and catalyze meaningful conversations and partnerships. Together, we'll navigate the path toward intelligent agents that don't just automate tasks, but actively empower individuals, enrich societies, and amplify human potential—setting the stage for a robust, safe, and transformative AI-powered future.

Date:   August 2, 2025

Venue:   UC Berkeley Campus, Berkeley, CA

Note that for access, registration approval is required for security purposes.

We invite proposals for presentations that align with our summit's mission and themes, exploring innovative ideas, groundbreaking research, real-world applications, and critical discussions around Agentic AI. Submit your talk proposals by May 15, 2025, and join us in shaping this critical conversation. We look forward to your proposals.


Summit Highlights

  • Visionary Keynotes - Hear directly from founders and leaders of pioneering AI organizations alongside distinguished academic luminaries
  • Expert Panel Discussions - Engage with thought leaders as they explore emerging trends and critical challenges in Agentic AI
  • Technical Workshops - Participate in hands-on workshops led by engineering leaders from leading AI companies
  • AgentX Demo Day - Witness cutting-edge innovations from finalists of the AgentX Competition demonstrating the future of Agentic AI
  • Networking - Build valuable connections during dedicated networking sessions and our evening reception
More details on schedule, speakers and sponsors will be announced soon.


The Summit is hosted by Berkeley RDI, a multi-disciplinary campus-wide center, focusing on advancing the science and technology of decentralization and decentralized intelligence, to make it universally accessible and empower a responsible digital economy.
